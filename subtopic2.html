Do electronic medical records improve quality of care?

URL Sourece Link
Site Author Donna P. Manca
Abstract (100–150 words) Electronic health records have had such an impact on healthcare in a positive way as technology keeps on improving. Compared to a paper chart for a patient an electronic health record opens up many more doors. Being able to hold information that a paper chart can’t an EHR is able to hold all of the patients data as well as descriptions for each illness or any abnormalities in the patient's chart history. This helps to give both the provider and the patient better understandings of what is going on and create a good patient plan moving forward in treatment processes.

7 Key Benefits of EHR Systems

URL Source Link
Site Author Bouve Communications
Abstract (100–150 words) With an electronic health record a patient's medical history is able to improve and gain easier access for both the patient and provider. There are many different ways that the EHR has been able to improve healthcare, it improves patients' education, understanding and knowledge. It also helps to keep patients up to date with all of their medical needs as well as an easy way to contact them. Being able to contact a provider through an electronic health record system gives the patient quick and upfront answers. All of these things are very important especially in today's society reducing miscommunication between patient and provider.
